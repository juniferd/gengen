In the style of Anni Albers

Anni Albers was a textile artist. I went down a rabbit hole reading and watching videos on the jacquard loom with its punch card system that makes it a predecessor to modern computing. this exercise takes the js Date object to construct a series of numbers in binary and prints them out. it will forever be changing since one of the numbers is the ever increasing UNIX epoch

i wasn't sure how to keep in the spirit of warp and weft (woof?)/over & under that's the key to weaving. settled on just constantly layering semi-transparent rectangles of variable heights over and over such that some of the previous layers show through.

the other more static image optionally renders your lat/long in binary if your browser supports the GeoLocation API and you enable it

built using js + canvas
